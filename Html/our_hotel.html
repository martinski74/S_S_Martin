The Hotel

Hotel Energy is located in the center of Kiten, only 50 meters from the south beach. The hotel is open for guests in 2006 and the quality services are a prerequisite for a complete and unforgetable holiday. The hotel offers 2 single, 14 double, 6 tripple rooms and 3 suites. The rooms and suites are equipped with bathroom, cable TV, refrigerator, air conditioner, safe and its own terrase overlooking the sea. There are 30 seats indoor and 60 seats of the summer terrase in the restaurant. There is a place, where you can enjoy the traditional Bulgarian cuisine, pizzas, fish, and sea delicacies. All this is combined with excellent services and affordable prices!
